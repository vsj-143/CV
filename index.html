Hinata

wife of NarutoUzmaki

Hinata is the oldest daughter of Hiashi Hyūga. When she was still a child, Hinata was almost kidnapped by
the-then Head Ninja of Kumogakure, who was visiting Konoha under the guise of signing a peace treaty between
their two villages
